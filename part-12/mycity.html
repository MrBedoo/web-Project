About Tokat

Tokat is a city in northern Turkey, known for its rich history and beautiful landscapes. From ancient castles to stunning caves, Tokat offers a variety of attractions for visitors. First of all you probably didn't even hear this city so I will begin telling some basic formal informations. Tokat is a province located in the Black Sea Region of Turkey. It is neighboring Samsun in the north, Ordu in the northeast, Sivas in the east and south, Yozgat in the southwest and Amasya in the west. One of the districts of Yesilyurt and Sulusaray remains in the Central Anatolian Region.
